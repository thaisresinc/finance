The golden rule
"If the invoice is not in the inbox by Monday morning, 38 hours gets paid. Full stop. No chasing, no waiting, no exceptions. If the electrician submits the invoice in a later cycle, it gets processed in the next cycle after it is received — through the accounts email, never in the master sheet."
