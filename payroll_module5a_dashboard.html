Critical rules
Do not enter without approval
Do not enter bonus amounts for employees who do not have the red bonus indicator on the master sheet — even if they appear in the bonus email. If someone appears in the email but not on the indicator list, confirm with the relevant manager before proceeding.
Wait for Stacey's approval
The bonus email must be approved by Stacey before any amounts are entered. If you receive the email from Casey or Jordan but it has not been confirmed as approved by Stacey, do not proceed — contact Casey or Jordan to confirm.
No email by Monday AM
If the bonus email has not arrived by Monday morning, contact Casey or Jordan directly. Do not assume zero bonus — always confirm before processing.
