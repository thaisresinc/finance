The big picture
19 electricians. Every Monday. Before anything else.
Resinc's electricians don't use Bright HR like everyone else. They submit a weekly invoice by email. Your job is to turn that invoice into an approved hours number — every Monday morning, before you touch anything else in payroll.

Resinc rule — the golden one
"If the invoice isn't in the inbox by Monday morning, 38 hours gets paid. Full stop. No chasing, no waiting, no exceptions. If they submit late, we correct it the following week — through the accounts email, never in the master sheet."
