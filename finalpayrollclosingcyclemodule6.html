Payroll onboarding
Closing the cycle
Tuesday through Friday. From sending the master sheet to accounts through to salaries landing. This is the final stretch — every step has a hard deadline.
What happens and when
Four days, four owners. Every step depends on the one before it.
Tuesday
Cheene sends master sheet to accounts
All sections must be complete. Attach the master sheet and super forms for any new starters. Use the payroll email template.
Wednesday
Accounts review, add PAYG + super, load Xero
Accounts adds PAYG and superannuation, resolves leave balance queries, loads all data into Xero, generates the ABA batch file, and sends it back to Jacqui.
Thursday AM
Jacqui compares figures and uploads to Commonwealth Bank
Jacqui compares the ABA batch file against the master sheet. If figures match, she uploads the batch file to Commonwealth Bank and notifies Leigh.
Thursday
Leigh approves in Commonwealth Bank
Leigh reviews and approves the payment.
Before 4pm Thursday — hard deadline
Friday
Salaries land in employee accounts
No fixed time. Cycle complete.
Pre-send checklist — tick each item
Complete every item before sending the master sheet to accounts. Accounts cannot process an incomplete sheet.
0 of 7 complete
Email to accounts — what to include
Open the payroll email template and update these fields manually before sending.
Annual and sick leave for GMs
Current week's leave for General Managers
GMs are not in the master sheet — their leave must be communicated separately in the email.
Contract changes
Any pay rate changes or contract updates effective this week
Include employee name, old rate, new rate, and effective date.
New employee details
Full name and start date for anyone starting this cycle
Even if already added to the master sheet — confirm in the email too.
Terminated employee details
Full name, last working date, and reason (dismissal or voluntary)
Include even if the row is already marked red in the master sheet.
Prior-week corrections (if any)
List each correction separately — employee name, week it relates to, adjustment amount, reason
Always at the end of the email, clearly separated from current week figures. Never add corrections directly to the master sheet.
Do not send until complete
Do not send the email until all sections of the master sheet have been completed. Accounts cannot process an incomplete sheet — and an incomplete submission puts the Thursday deadline at risk.
If something goes wrong
Three scenarios — know what to do before they happen.
Leigh unavailable Thursday
Payment cannot be approved before 4pm
Action
Contact Jacqui
Contact Jacqui immediately — do not submit the payment without an authorised approver. Even a short delay is better than submitting without approval.
Payment error after submission
Commonwealth Bank flags an issue
Action
Contact Jacqui
Jacqui receives an email notification from Commonwealth Bank (e.g. BSB and account number do not match). She contacts the employee to confirm correct bank details, updates Xero, then processes a fast payment Saturday if urgent — or Monday if not.
Accounts sheet not returned
No sheet back from accounts by Wednesday
Action
Follow up now
Follow up with accounts immediately — Wednesday is the last day before the Thursday upload deadline. Do not wait to see if it arrives. If no response, escalate to Jacqui.
