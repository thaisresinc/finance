Verifying the pay rate
Two places to look — only one is the source of truth.
Source of truth
Signed contract PDF
Documents tab → employment contract → last page. Always use this rate.
Secondary only
Employment tab → Pay details
May be out of date. Flag it if the two figures differ.
The rule
"If the contract and Bright HR show different rates, always use the contract. If something looks off, flag it before entering anything — don't guess."
